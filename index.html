Explore Host Cities

New York / New Jersey

MetLife Stadium is one of the most iconic and modern venues in the New York metropolitan area,renowned for hosting world-class sports and entertainment. This multi-billion dollar stadium serves as the proud home ground for two famous NFL teams, the New York Giants and the New York Jets. It has also been selected as the historic venue to host the grand final match of the FIFA World Cup 2026.

  • Massive seating capacity
  • Expensive construction cost
  • World Cup host
  • Multi-purpose event venue
  • Iconic football home

Mexico City

Mexico City is a vibrant, high-altitude metropolis known for its rich blend of ancient Aztec history and modern urban culture. As the capital of Mexico and one of the largest cities in North America, it serves as a major economic and cultural hub. The city will also play a key role in the FIFA World Cup 2026, hosting the opening match at the historic Estadio Azteca.

  • Rich Aztec history
  • Massive population hub
  • High altitude location
  • Vibrant cultural scene
  • Estadio Azteca venue

Toronto

Toronto is Canada's largest city and a dynamic global metropolis celebrated for its remarkable cultural diversity and striking modern architecture. As the nation's financial and economic heart, it stands out as a safe, clean, and highly livable urban center. The city is also gearing up as a major host venue for the highly anticipated FIFA World Cup 2026 matches.

  • Canada's largest city
  • Diverse cultural population
  • Major financial hub
  • CN Tower landmark
  • World Cup host

Vancouver

Vancouver is a stunning Canadian coastal city renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty, where majestic mountains meet the Pacific Ocean. Known for its high quality of life, the city seamlessly blends a bustling, modern urban center with rich ethnic diversity and green living. It will also be a key host city for the FIFA World Cup 2026, welcoming global football fans to BC Place.

  • Stunning coastal beauty
  • Mild oceanic climate
  • Diverse multicultural community
  • Active outdoor lifestyle
  • Active outdoor lifestyle
